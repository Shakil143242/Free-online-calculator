About Body Fat Percentage

What is Body Fat Percentage?

Body fat percentage is the total mass of fat divided by total body mass, multiplied by 100. It includes both essential fat and storage fat.

Unlike BMI, body fat percentage distinguishes between fat mass and lean mass, providing a more accurate health assessment.

Why It Matters

Knowing your body fat percentage can help you:

  • Assess your health risks more accurately than BMI
  • Track fitness progress more effectively
  • Set appropriate weight management goals
  • Understand your body composition better

Measurement Methods

Several methods exist for measuring body fat:

  • Skinfold calipers (anthropometric method)
  • Bioelectrical impedance analysis (BIA)
  • Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA)
  • Hydrostatic weighing
  • U.S. Navy circumference method (used in this calculator)
